Livria - Application

Development - Visual identity - Communication

Livria is a cross-platform application. The purpose of Livria is to facilitate the exchange of books between individuals. The application is available on Google Play Store and Apple Store.
Originally a student project, I had the opportunity to continue to work on it during my end of studies internship. I developped the front side of the application, and also created the identity (logo and color pallet) and visuals for communication.

Softwares : Adobe Illustrator - Adobe Photoshop - Affinity Designer
Development : React Native - Expo - Firebase - Algolia
